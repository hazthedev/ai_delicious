# Sunny Garden — Rebrand Plan (replacing Ai Delicious template)

> **Project brief:** Repurpose the existing Ai Delicious MVP (`ai-delicious-sigma.vercel.app`) as a landing site for **Sunny Garden 三合园**, a halal Chinese-Muslim restaurant in PV128 Setapak (near Wangsa Maju). Same template structure (boutique cream + gold elegance, family-cafe DNA). Cuisine swaps from Chinese fusion → halal Chinese dumpling specialist.
>
> **Why this swap works:** Both brands are Chinese-cuisine cafes targeting families and couples with a curated aesthetic. Both have signature dishes (Ai Delicious's croizza-style fusion → Sunny Garden's signature dumplings). Both use similar elegant photography styles. The Ai Delicious cream + gold palette translates perfectly to a Chinese-Muslim heritage feel — just shift the gold slightly toward red-gold for traditional Chinese tone.

## 2. Design Direction (mapping from Ai Delicious template)

### Color Palette (refined from Ai Delicious's cream + gold)

| Role | Ai Delicious Original | Sunny Garden Replacement | Hex |
|------|----------------------|--------------------------|-----|
| Background (primary) | Warm Cream | Warm Cream | `#FAF6F0` (keep) |
| Surface | Pure White | Pure White | `#FFFFFF` (keep) |
| Primary text | Deep Charcoal | Ink Black | `#2B2420` (keep) |
| **Accent (THE color)** | Bronze Gold `#B08D57` | **Red-Gold (中国红金)** | `#B5391F` |
| Secondary accent | Sage Green | Bamboo Green | `#5D7A4E` |
| Highlight | Pure White | Egg-Yolk Yellow (盈黄) | `#E8B838` |
| Muted | Warm Taupe | Warm Taupe | `#A89885` (keep) |

**Why these:**
- **Red-gold** — traditional Chinese auspicious color (中国红), matches the heritage brand
- **Bamboo green** — subtle nod to chives (韭菜) used in their signature dumplings
- **Egg-yolk yellow** — the steaming dumpling skin color, warm-comfort
- **Cream + black** structure stays — keeps the elegant editorial feel

⚠️ **Important:** Don't overdo the "Chinese restaurant red." This brand is *humble Chinese-Muslim*, not loud Cantonese banquet. Use red-gold sparingly and tastefully — accents only, not whole-section backgrounds.

### Typography (keep Ai Delicious's choices, add Chinese accents)

- **Display / Headings:** `Fraunces` (keep — same elegant editorial energy)
- **Body:** `Inter` (keep)
- **Chinese accent:** `Noto Serif SC` for the brand mark "三合园" (Sān Hé Yuán) — used sparingly like Buriram's "泰国小小碗"
- **Numerals (prices):** `Inter` (keep)
- **Accent / handwritten:** `Caveat` for "since 2013" annotations

### Mood

Halal-Chinese-Heritage · Hidden-Gem · Family-Run · Dumpling-Forward · Humble-Premium · Bilingual

Visual references: traditional Chinese-Muslim restaurants, Xinjiang cuisine photography, dumpling close-ups in flower arrangements, warm-cream ceramic plates with red accents. NOT: loud Cantonese banquet aesthetics, generic chain Chinese, hipster fusion.
